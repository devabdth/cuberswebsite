Our Vision & Purposes!

 Our vision and mission is an amalgamation of our corporate philosophy and our motto of providing next generation Educational Systems, To train a large scaled group of juniors for the labor time; To increase the total quality of the Egyptian market.

 Quality, Trainee Satisfaction, Up-to-Date Curriculums and Well-planned educational timelines are the pillars of our vision and mission and form the cornerstone of our approach that we bring it every engagement.

How we became this?

Cubers Academy is an Egyptian Company that is sub-branded from Cubers IO. We Cubers Academy, seeking to prepare youth aged from 17 ~ 27 years for the labor market by giving them: One-Day Workshops, Two-Days Workshops, Three-Days Workshops, Bootcamps, Courses, Diplomas. After our trainees finishes the programs they’ve chosen we take the highest ranked trainee and give internship in our main brand: Cubers IO.

 We’re working in changing the educational business in Egypt by turning it from one-side benefit business - Only for the Institutions - into a win-win situation as the trainee must have the a real life opportunity.

 Our Experience in training juniors in our main brand make us ready for establishing the new sub-brand to make the circle the really benefits much wider.
